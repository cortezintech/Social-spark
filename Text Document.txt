SOCIAL SPARK
Social Spark is an app designed to help people who struggle with maintaining social connection during busy days.
The Social Spark feature lets you tie a specific time of the to a personal keywords (coffee,music,work) to trigger activities.
Key Features:
-Theme button(top right):switches the app theme
-Time of day*dropdown:choose a period(morning,afternoon,evening,night)
-Enter a word*textfield:input any word that represents an activity.
